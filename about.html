About Us

Who We Are

Founded by Steven Bunn, who studied Turf Grass Management at Clemson University, GVLOL’s experience is not limited to one just field — Steven also has experience with botanical gardening and garden design. After college, Steven started his own landscape company, but quickly realized the need for creative landscapers, which is why Greenville Outdoor Living’s mission is to better serve the Greenville community by offering a range of services, including Landscape Design, Installation, Hardscapes, Drainage, Irrigation and Maintenance, and more.

We love our clients, and partnering with them to see their dreams in greenspaces come to life!
